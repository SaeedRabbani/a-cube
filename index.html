About Us

We thrive on spreading joy & creating happy memories for our guests.

We take pride in bringing unparalleled recreational & dining experiences to our country. Some of the iconic attractions being operated by A-Cube include Dino Valley, Ayubia Chairlift, Monal restaurants, M-1 Traxx, Asian Wok restaurants, Monal Fusion Food Mall and Monal Marquees.

Following are some of our iconic achievements:

  • Dino Valley is the largest & first of its kind interactive animatronic Dinosaur theme park in Pakistan.
  • ⁠Under our Monal, AsianWok & Monal Fusion brands we operate the largest chain of restaurants in Pakistan.
  • ⁠We run the first & only privately operated chairlift in Pakistan.
  • We cater to over 8 million customers annually which puts us almost as per with the number of people visiting Universal Studios in Orlando, Florida or even Disneyland in Paris.

Founding Members

ALI ABBAS SYED

Ali Abbas Syed has over 21 years of Business Development and Management experience. His expertise lies in trade and structured finance. He has successfully negotiated and restructured business operations with Governments, Banks and Financial Institutions in the US, UK, Singapore, Switzerland, the GCC and Pakistan in his role as the Managing Director at Cargill Inc (www.cargill.com) He holds an MPA / ID from the Kennedy School of Government and Harvard Business School, and an MSc in Economics from Oxford University.

LUQMAN ALI AFZAL

Luqman Ali Afzal has 23 years of experience in the hospitality industry. He has led the way by pioneering unique dining and recreational experiences that have become landmarks in Pakistan. His expertise lies in developing and managing specialized teams that are committed to creating an unparalleled customer experience. His contributions to the country's hospitality ecosystem have been recognised by the Presidential Pride of Performance that he was awarded for entreprenurship in 2019, and the the Presidential Pride for Excellence in Hospitality Services that he was awarded on the recommendation of the ICCI in 2021.

Islamabad


Monal Pir Sohawa, nestled on the scenic Margalla Hills, offers breathtaking views alongside a diverse menu of Pakistani and international dishes, delighting all guests.
M1 Traxx, is the perfect destination for outdoor enthusiasts. Enjoy an action-packed experience with a variety of exciting rides offering fun for friends and families.
Asian Wok at Beverly Centre, brings you the finest Chinese, Thai, and Japanese cuisines. Enjoy authentic Pan-Asian dishes with exceptional service in the heart of the twin cities.
Monal Caterers and Event Managers, provide top-notch outdoor catering services in the Twin Cities and its surroundings. Enjoy captivating decorations to make your gatherings unforgettable.

Rawalpindi


Monal Rawalpindi: Monal Rawalpindi, located in bustling Saddar, offers indoor and outdoor dining with panoramic city views. Experience a contemporary restaurant serving exquisite global cuisines with a fusion of traditional and innovative flavors.
Asian Wok Bahria Town: Asian Wok Bahria Town Rawalpindi offers a delightful culinary journey through Chinese, Thai, and Japanese cuisines in Phase 7. Indulge in authentic Pan-Asian dishes and exceptional service for an unmatched dining experience.

Murree


Asian Wok Murree: located at Park Ridge Plaza on the Islamabad-Murree Expressway, offers delectable Chinese, Thai, and Japanese cuisine. Enjoy hearty meals and warm, comforting dishes amidst the cool, refreshing Murree weather.

Haripur


Dino Valley: Dino Valley, a one-of-a-kind dinosaur-themed park, is nestled among stunning hills and is a must-visit destination for families in the twin cities. Experience life-sized, animatronic dinosaurs brought to life by motion sensors, indulge in immersive games and activities and relish delicious cuisine. Dino Valley stands out not just as a unique amusement park in Islamabad, but as an exceptional adventure across Pakistan.!

Ayubia


Ayubia: Dino Valley Ayubia, nestled in the picturesque hills of Ayubia, Pakistan, promises an unforgettable experience for families. Savor mouthwatering cuisine while exploring the park's chairlift, offering breathtaking views and winter snow experiences. Dino Valley is poised to be a must-visit destination and a unique adventure across the country. Coming soon!
